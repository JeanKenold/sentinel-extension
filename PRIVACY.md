# Privacy Policy for Sentinel

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

Sentinel ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data when you use our Chrome Extension.

### 1. Data Collection and Transmission
Sentinel follows a **Zero-Knowledge** architecture. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal, financial, or authentication data to external servers. All data auditing and validation processes occur strictly on the user's local machine.

### 2. Data Usage
The extension processes the following data types locally to provide real-time security alerts:
- **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**: Emails and IDs.
- **Financial Information**: Credit card numbers.
- **Authentication Information**: API Keys and Tokens.
- **Website Content**: Text within input fields for auditing purposes.

### 3. Third-Party Access
Since no data is collected, no data is ever shared with or sold to third parties.
